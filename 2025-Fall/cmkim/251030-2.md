### Objective
Identifing **surface-expressed (surfaceome/secretome) genes** in the complete genomes of *Staphylococcus epidermidis*.  
This analysis integrates **annotation-based**, **sequence-based**, and **experimental (MS-based, published)** approaches to comprehensively detect surface proteins.

### Results

#### 1. Annotation-based approach
- Added **GO annotation filtering** using the Gene Ontology term **GO:0005618** (*cell wall* / *cell surface*)  
- Genes annotated with this GO term were classified as surface-associated proteins.  
- Few proteins were classified by this approach.

#### 2. Sequence-based approach
Surface protein prediction was additionally performed using multiple computational tools:
| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **TMHMM** | Predicts transmembrane helices to identify membrane-associated proteins |
| **Phobius** | Predicts both transmembrane regions and signal peptides |
| **SignalP**, **GPApred**, and **DeepLocPro**  | from previous step |

#### 3. Experimental validation reference
